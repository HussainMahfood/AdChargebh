Mobile Charging Stations and Solutions

Our daily life became more dependent on mobile phones. These mobile phones are no longer used merely for calls but rather to perform many daily tasks. From basic things like sending and receiving emails to socializing with family and friends using social media and applications to even more complex tasks like performing bank transactions and accessing government services. We became more dependent on mobile to the extent that we feel disconnected and start to panic when the mobile runs out of charge.
Providing the charging service in venues and public places like shopping malls, restaurants and cafes, events exhibitions and trade shows, conventions centers, medical centers and hospitals, university campuses and colleges, airports etc. has many advantages such as:
  • Provide a value added service that lots of people need
  • Keep people powered and stay connected on the go
  • Keep customers, veterans and visitors happy and loyal to the business
  • Keep recurrent customers and attract new ones
  • Increase foot traffic and dwell time
  • Engage customers and promote your products, brands and services
  • Enhance customers stay and experience
AdCharge is the sole provider that is specialized in mobile charging solutions in Bahrain. It has been in this business since 2014. It provides many types of charging stations and solutions for businesses to satisfy the charging requirements of customers, clients and staff. These solutions are bundled with branding, advertising to enable promote the brands, products, and services while providing a value added service.
