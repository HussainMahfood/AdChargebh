Digital Signage Solutions
Whether you are a shopping mall, company, hospital, university, or any type of business you know how important it is to engage your customers, staff and visitors on a daily basis. There is lots of information distributed every day. People want to hear about everything from what is happening around them to announcements to schedules to updates. Administrators and people responsible for getting that information out often find it challenging to get relevant information to staff and visitors without also overloading them with info they do not need.

Digital Signage gives the durability and flexibility needed to communicate with customers, staff, students, visitors etc. in buildings. It is a centrally controlled, content distribution platform by which to playback digital content to one or many displays or screens. Digital Signage screens can be used for the relaying of any number of types of information; live weather, news, TV, menus, flights, calendars, adverts for example. It can be found in both public and private environments, including shopping malls, university campuses, schools, medical facilities, airports, train and bus stations, banks, auto dealerships, retail stores, hotels, restaurants, exhibitions, libraries and corporate buildings, amongst other locations.
AdCharge is providing Digital Signage solutions for indoor and outdoor with LED screen sizes from 10 inch onward. Wall mounted or floor standing, or even modular based LED screens, AdCharge can satisfy your needs and provide you the best digital signage solution that caters for your requirements.

GPS Tracking and Fleet Management System
GPS tracking system uses the Global Positioning System (GPS) to track and locate vehicles. It is an invaluable tool for monitoring either a single car, motorbike or an entire fleet of vehicles. A real-time tracking device gives up-to-the-minute speed and location updates. GPS tracking technology is the cornerstone of successful fleet management. It is an insightful, effective way for fleet owners and managers to monitor their cars, trucks or vehicles and support fleet drivers. Customers, fleet employees and vehicles can all benefit from a GPS tracking system; reduced costs, improved delivery times, driver accountability, and maintenance alerts are just a few advantages to count.

The system utilizes Google Maps, which is a very useful interface with the ability to Draw Geography fence on a map to secure any wished area. A GPS tracking device is installed in each vehicle to track its location in Real-time. Location tracking can be monitored using PC web interface or Mobile/Tablet Application.
